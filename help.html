About

Quest was designed to help users with ADHD complete tasks, aka Quests. Unlike traditional to-do lists, Quest makes use of a 'gamified' system to provide users with a sense of reward for accomplishing quests. Users are rewarded for things like completing a quests, planning quests and completing every quest in their planner.

How to Quest

Start by using the Quest planner to add tasks to your list, from there you can visit Begin Questing to start using the app. The logs page will show you the average time taken to complete quests as well as the average number of quests completed per day, along with every quest that has been completed. Rewards are shown directly on the homepage for users when they log in.
